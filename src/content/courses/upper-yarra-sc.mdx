Located in the beautiful Yarra Ranges, **Upper Yarra Secondary College** is home to a 9 hole disc golf course. It's part of the school's physical education curriculum, and is available for all students (and staff) in their free time.

The course was designed to fit in with the existing activities on the school grounds, including a vineyard. The course layout weaves between two main ovals, making use of the tree lined areas and elevation in the hilly ranges.

## Availability

Despite being on a school campus, the course is accessible to the public after school hours.
  - weekdays after 3:40pm
  - anytime on weekends

## Features

- 9 holes
- "how to play" signage for new players

## Facilities

- water at the nearby school playground

## Getting there

### By car

- Parking is plentiful on Little Yarra Road, or in accessible school carparks.

### By public transport

- **Train** (**Lilydale** station)
- **Bus** (route **683**) - 15 minute walk from bus stop
