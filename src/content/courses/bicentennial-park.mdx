The course at Bicentennial Park offers a combination of wooded holes that will test your accuracy, and more open holes where you can "let it rip".
It's a great challenge for both new and experienced players.

## Features

- 18 baskets
- 9 concrete tees
- practice basket near hole 1

## Facilities

- plenty of car parking
- toilets
- playground
- skatepark
- netball/basketball courts
