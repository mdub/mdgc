A beginner-friendly disc golf course designed to provide a complete disc golf experience for players of all skill levels.

## Course Features
- **9 baskets**
- **18 tees**
- **Concrete tee pads**
- **RAD baskets**
- **Full course signage**
- **Practice basket area** with "how to play" sign for new players

## Course Design
Designed specifically to be beginner-friendly, Bald Hill Park provides a complete disc golf experience for players of all skill levels. The practice area is specifically designed to help new players learn the fundamentals of the game.
