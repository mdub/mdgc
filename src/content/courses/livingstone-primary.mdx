A perfect course for ace runs and practicing short game, featuring tight hyzer/anhyzer lines ideal for introducing new players to disc golf.

## Course Features
- **6 baskets**
- **Short holes**
- **Tight hyzer/anhyzer lines**

## Ideal For
- **Ace runs**
- **Practicing short game**
- **Introducing new players to disc golf**

## Course Management
The course features a check-in sign installed near the 2nd hole for usage tracking, and is supported by Whitehorse City Council. This school-based course provides an excellent introduction to disc golf in a safe, controlled environment.
