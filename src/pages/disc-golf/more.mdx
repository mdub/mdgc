## Major championships

Professional disc golf has several major championships throughout the year:

**PDGA World Championships** - The most prestigious event, crowning the world champions in various divisions.

**United States Disc Golf Championship** - One of the oldest and most respected tournaments.

**European Open** - The premier European tournament, known for challenging wooded courses.

**Disc Golf Pro Tour Championship** - Season-ending championship featuring the year's best players.

## Learning from the pros

Watching professional disc golf isn't just entertainment - it's education:

- Study their throwing techniques and form
- Learn course management and shot selection
- Observe how they handle pressure situations
- Watch their pre-shot routines and mental approach
- See how different disc selections work in various situations

## Australian disc golf

The Australian disc golf scene is growing rapidly:

**Australian Disc Golf Championships** - The premier Australian tournament, attracting top players from around the country.

**Victorian Disc Golf Championships** - Our state championship event.

Follow Australian disc golf on social media and YouTube to stay connected with the local scene.

</Text>
