## Educational Integration

The course provides innovative recreational and educational opportunities for the school community, allowing students and staff to engage with disc golf during the school day and breaks, promoting physical activity and introducing a new sport to young people.

## Innovation in Education

This course represents a forward-thinking approach to physical education, demonstrating how disc golf can be successfully integrated into school curricula to provide students with new recreational opportunities and promote active lifestyles.
